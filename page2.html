HISTORY
Hot pot, ancient known as "antique soup".
Named for the "goo-dong" sound made when food is thrown
into boiling water, it is one of the original Chinese delicacies
and is also a food suitable for all ages. Hot pot is not only a way of cooking,but also a symbol of culture.
Hot pot generally refers to a pot as an instrument, a heat
source to boil the pot, water or soup to boil all kinds of food
cooking methods.
It can also refer to the cooking utensils used in this way. It is
characterized by cooking while eating, or the pot itself has a
thermal insulation effect. Eat hot pot if you have gout then you
can drink with low food to prevent gout recurrence. Hot pot is
hot to eat, spicy salty, oil and not greasy, relief of dampness,
suitable for the climate of mountains and rivers, today's
development into a mandarin duck pot, spicy, light, each takes
what he needs, according to personal preferences with
different soup ingredients, food, suitable for all ages, to winter.
Typical hot pot ingredients include a variety of meat, seafood,
vegetables, soy products, mushrooms, egg products, staple
foods, etc., which are boiled in water or the bottom of a special
stock pot after boiling. Some recipes are also served with
condiments.
